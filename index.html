Robert L. Manuel is an accomplished, transformative leader who brings 30 years of academic excellence in higher education to DePaul University. Throughout his career, he has elevated the impact of the institutions he has served to create life-changing opportunities for students. Rob joins DePaul at a critical moment of institutional change and optimism for the nation’s largest Catholic university.
